Article I — Product Specification

Fixed-rate, unsecured installment credit. No add-on fees.

This instrument provides for an unsecured personal installment loan with a principal amount between $500 and $15,000, an annual percentage rate between 5.99% and 35.99%, and a term between 3 and 60 months. The product is closed-end credit within the meaning of Regulation Z § 1026.2(a)(10).

The annual percentage rate is fixed at origination and does not vary over the life of the loan. The monthly payment is computed under the standard amortization formula M = P · r / (1 − (1 + r)−n), where P is the principal, r the periodic rate, and n the term in months.

Each tier reflects the maximum APR offered for that FICO band. Underwriting may yield a lower rate; it will not yield a higher one. — UW · 04.28
