01 / Story

The cost of borrowing should be a number, not a feeling.

The last decade of consumer-finance marketing taught a generation of borrowers to expect a soft credit pull, a rate "estimate," and a bait-and-switch on the offer page. We started Get A Cash Advance Today because we wanted to lend money the way the textbooks describe it: a principal, a term, an APR, and a fixed monthly payment. No mid-application repricing. No origination percentage skimmed off the top.

Our entire offer matrix lives on this homepage. If your FICO score lands in the Excellent band, you'll see 5.99%. If it lands in Building, you'll see 35.99% — the federal-law ceiling for an installment loan and the rate beyond which we will not lend. The four tiers in between are not negotiable, are not modified by undisclosed factors, and are the same numbers every loan officer at this company sees on their underwriting screen.

This is not the cheapest possible product. It is, however, the most readable one.

02 / Process

From slider to settlement, in three movements.

STEP 01

Adjust the math

Move the principal and term sliders below. Pick a credit-tier band. Your monthly payment, finance charge, and total of payments compute live, in your browser, using the standard amortization formula.

STEP 02

Submit the form

Six fields, one screen, six minutes. We pull credit once, at the moment you submit — never the soft-pull-then-hard-pull surprise. ECOA Regulation B applies; an adverse-action notice arrives within 24 hours of decline.

STEP 03

Receive funds

Same-day or next-business-day ACH credit on approval. Fixed monthly debits begin thirty days after funding. Pay it off any day, save the unaccrued interest. We do not charge to pay early.
