§ 01 / Acceptance

Acceptance & eligibility

By submitting an application, signing a loan agreement, or otherwise accessing or using the services offered at getacashadvancetoday.com, you agree to be bound by these Terms & Conditions in their entirety. If you do not agree to any provision, you must close this page and refrain from submitting personal information through the application form.

To be eligible for a loan you must be at least eighteen (18) years of age, a citizen or lawful permanent resident of the United States, hold a valid U.S. checking or savings account in your own name capable of receiving an inbound ACH credit, possess a valid Social Security number, and reside in a U.S. state in which we are licensed to lend at the rates and terms applicable to your application.

§ 02 / Product

The loan product

Get A Cash Advance Today underwrites fixed-rate, unsecured personal installment loans with simple-interest amortization, monthly payment frequency, and no balloon, no insurance bundling, no add-on fees, and no prepayment penalty. Principal $500–$15,000; term 3–60 months; APR 5.99–35.99% inclusive of all finance charges.

The loan is a closed-end credit product within the meaning of Regulation Z § 1026.2(a)(10): the disbursed principal is fixed at the outset, the repayment schedule is fixed at the outset, and the contract terminates upon receipt of the final scheduled payment or sooner upon prepayment in full.

§ 03 / APR formula

APR, finance charge, monthly payment

Your annual percentage rate is determined at underwriting using the rate-tier matrix published in Article I; the four tiers are Excellent (740+ FICO — 5.99% APR), Good (680–739 — 14.99%), Fair (620–679 — 24.99%) and Building (below 620 — 35.99%).

The monthly payment M is computed using the standard amortization formula M = P · r / (1 − (1 + r)−n), where P is the principal, r is the periodic rate (APR ÷ 12 ÷ 100), and n is the term in months. The total of payments is M · n; the finance charge is the total of payments minus the principal.

§ 04 / Underwriting

Application & underwriting

The application collects (1) contact details, (2) income and ZIP, and (3) FCRA authorization. We perform automated identity verification, OFAC sanctions screening and CIP checks under the USA PATRIOT Act § 326. A single human underwriter reviews each completed application and renders a credit decision within 24 hours of submission. The decision is governed by the published rate-tier matrix; we do not use undisclosed pricing factors.

§ 05 / FCRA

FCRA hard-pull authorization

By ticking the FCRA-authorization box on Form GACT-APP, you authorize Get A Cash Advance Today to obtain one or more hard-pull consumer reports from a nationwide consumer-reporting agency for the permissible purpose of credit underwriting under 15 U.S.C. § 1681b(a)(3)(A). The hard-pull will appear on your consumer report and may temporarily depress your credit score in line with the bureau's scoring model.

You may withdraw this authorization at any time before submission; once submitted, the authorization is irrevocable for the duration necessary to complete the underwriting decision and the related adverse-action notice procedures.

§ 06 / Reg B

Adverse-action (Equal Credit Opportunity Act — Regulation B)

If your application is declined, in whole or in part, the Equal Credit Opportunity Act and Regulation B (12 C.F.R. § 1002.9) require us to send you, within thirty days of receiving a complete application, a written adverse-action notice stating the action taken, the principal reason(s) for the adverse action, the name and contact information of the consumer-reporting agency that supplied any consumer report we relied upon, and the federal anti-discrimination notice prescribed by the regulation.

§ 07 / ACH

Funding & ACH authorization

If approved, you authorize Get A Cash Advance Today to initiate an inbound ACH credit of the principal amount to the U.S. checking or savings account whose routing and account numbers you provided at the application. You also authorize recurring outbound ACH debits of the scheduled monthly payment, on the due dates listed in the amortization schedule annexed to your loan agreement, until the loan is paid in full.

You may revoke the recurring-debit authorization by giving us written notice no fewer than three business days before the next scheduled debit; revocation does not cancel the underlying loan obligation.

§ 08 / Repayment

Repayment schedule

Each loan is repaid in n equal monthly installments. Installments are due on the same calendar date each month, beginning thirty days after the funding date. The amortization schedule annexed to your loan agreement lists the specific calendar dates, the principal and interest split of each installment, and the running balance after each payment.

§ 09 / Default

Late charges & default

An installment is considered late if not paid in full by the close of business on the calendar day ten days after its due date. A flat $15 late charge accrues on each late installment. We will waive the late charge on a borrower's first occurrence of lateness if you contact support before the due date and arrange a deferral or partial-payment plan in writing.

An installment that remains unpaid 60 days after its due date constitutes an event of default, at which point the entire remaining principal balance, accrued interest and accrued late charges become immediately due and payable, and the loan is reported as charged off.

§ 10 / Prepayment

Prepayment

You may prepay any portion or the entire outstanding balance of your loan, at any time, without penalty. Interest is accrued on a simple-interest, daily-pro-rata basis on the outstanding principal balance, so a prepayment in excess of the scheduled installment will save you the unaccrued interest on the prepaid principal for the remainder of the loan term.

Part 1022). You may dispute the accuracy of any item we furnish by writing to us or to the relevant consumer-reporting agency directly.
